PSY4013 - Intro to Social Psychology

Seminar 2 Subtle prejudice

In this seminar, we will explore our personal reactions to different social situations and social groups. Then we will discuss where such reactions come from and why they matter.

2.3 Recommended readings and further resources

After the seminar, you might want to read this article (optional):

  • Meertens, R. W., & Pettigrew, T. F. (1997). Is subtle prejudice really prejudice?. The Public Opinion Quarterly, 61(1), 54-71.
